Ceviche

This lemony treat is a latin treat. It's perfect for those hot summer days

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of frozen tilapia
  • 1-2 pounds of limes
  • 1 red onion
  • 1 large cucumber
  • 2 roma tomatoes
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Take your thawed tilapia and place them evenly in a deep pan. Fill the pan with water and add salt to it. Boil the fish
  2. While the tilapia is cooking, dice your vegetables and cut the limes in half. Place all of the vegetables in a pot and hold the limes for now, you'll need them later
  3. Once your fish is fully cooked, take each piece at a time and squeeze the water out of it. When all of the water has been drained, take the fish and place it in the pot with the vegetables. Repeat this until all the fish is drained and in the pot.
  4. Squeeze all of the lime juice into the pot and mix until thoroughly mixed
  5. Serve and enjoy!
