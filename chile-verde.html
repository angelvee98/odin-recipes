Chile Verde

Chile Verde is a traditional mexican dish, made up of pork and delicious green salsa. The flavor profile is spicy and savory, and never disappoints!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds of tomateos
  • 1-3 chile serano peppers [depending on how spicy you like it]
  • 2-3 pounds of pork [your choice of cut]
  • 1 1/4 teaspoons of salt
  • 2 cloves of garlic

Steps

  1. Peel and wash your tomateos then place them in a big enough pot with just enough water to cover them. Place them on a burner until they boil, and wait until they're soft
  2. While your tomateos are boiling, take your pork and cut it into cubes.
  3. Lightly salt and cook your pork until light brown and all the juices have been absorbed into the pork. the trick is to cook it on high and gradually bring the temp down
  4. Get your blender and a slated spoon. Place all the tomateos in the blender and make sure that you also place a cup of the water along with them. Add your peppers, garlic cloves, and salt. Blend the ingredients until it is all liquid.
  5. Pour the salsa into the cooked pork. You will then mix it all together and let it simmer until lightly boiling.
  6. Pair with some red Mexican Rice and Enjoy!
