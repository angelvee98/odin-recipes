Pozole

This savory dish is perfect for a cold night and always perfect for the cold holidays.

Ingredients

  • 1-2 Pounds of chicken thighs and legs
  • Oregano
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic
  • Knorr chicken boulin seasoning
  • Chile California peppers
  • Water
  1. Get a small pot and open and deseed 13 chile California peppers. Once done, Throw them into the pot with water and let them boil.
  2. Get blender and take the peppers and water and throw them in there along with 1 1/2 teaspoons of oregano, 1 tablespoon of knorr chicken boulin seasoning, and 2 cloves of garlic. Blend until smooth
  3. Once blended, get a strainer and slowly pour the contents from the blender through the strainer into a large soup pot
  4. add 1 1/2 cups of water to the blender to get the rest of the broth materials and pour that through the strainer into the pot
  5. Pour the chicken into the pot and let the broth boil on medium until the chicken is cooked thoroughly
  6. Serve and enjoy!
